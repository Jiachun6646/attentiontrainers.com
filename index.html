Click the Color not the Word

A Mind-Bending Attention Training Challenge

Game Overview

Click the Color not the Word is a challenging cognitive training game designed to test and improve your mental focus, reaction time, and attention to detail.

How to Play

  1. The game presents a colored word that doesn't match its text
  2. Your task is to click the color, not read the word
  3. You have limited time to make the correct choice
  4. Progress through 20 increasingly difficult levels

Key Features

Cognitive Challenge

Tests your brain's ability to override automatic reading response

20 Levels

Progressive difficulty to keep you engaged and challenged

Quick Sessions

Each game takes just a few minutes to play

Scoring System

The game features a simple yet motivating scoring system:

  • 5 points for each successfully completed level
  • Maximum possible score: 100 points
  • Test your skills and try to beat your previous high score!

Cognitive Benefits

Regular play of Click the Color not the Word can help:

  • Improve cognitive flexibility
  • Enhance reaction times
  • Train selective attention
  • Challenge your brain's automatic response patterns
